This is my Mind, Body, and Soul Project

This is my Artist Statement

We were tasked with creating three 12’ by 12’ collages that show our mind, body and soul. The collage had to be done entirely with found photographs. One of the biggest issues that I faced was getting the cuts around the photos to work together without showing the original background. My vision for this project was very different from the finished result and I think that it for the best. I wanted my idea to be a starting point and not to be the only thing that I think of. I moved piece and removed photos where I saw issues and tried to make it flow as best as possible. This work was like nothing I have done before, and the entire thing was a learning process, but I think moving forward I will do more art pieces like this. This is a link to my Process Book
